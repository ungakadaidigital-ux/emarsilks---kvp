Our Heritage

Serving Kovilpatti For Over 100 Years

EMAR Silks & Readymades has been the only textile shop of its kind serving the needs of Kovilpatti and the wider southern belt of Tamil Nadu for more than a century. Built on one unwavering principle — best quality and reasonable price — the store has grown from a modest family business into a landmark showroom, without ever losing its founding promise.

Today EMAR sparkles with a fantastic infrastructure and dedicated sections for every member of the family, especially our signature silk saree collections. With a century of notable service behind us, we continue to carry a wide and varied range of fancy and embroidered sarees, along with an unbeatable selection of clothing for ladies, gentlemen, and children.
