Our Story

A Legacy Woven Since the 1920s

EMAR Silks & Readymades has served the people of Kovilpatti and the southern belt of Tamil Nadu for more than a century, guided by one enduring policy — best quality at a reasonable price. What began as a small textile store has grown into a landmark showroom trusted across generations.

Today, EMAR blends that heritage with a fresh, spacious showroom experience — dedicated sections for silk sarees, wedding wear, and family fashion — while keeping the personal warmth that has always set us apart.
